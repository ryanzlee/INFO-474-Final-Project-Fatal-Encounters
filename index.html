Fatal Encounters

With our project, we hope to provide more context on the scale of people that are killed during interactions with the police in the United States. There have been many instances where people have been killed by interactions with the police, but there is always some lack of context such as where, how often, and what circumstances cause law enforcement to use deadly force.
Source: Fatal Encounters Database
